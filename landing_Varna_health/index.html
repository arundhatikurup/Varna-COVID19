Varna

Varna saves lives by giving hospitals a quick and easy way to screen large groups of healthcare workers and proactively manage symptoms.





Why Varna?

Research in the UK demonstrates that up to 16% of health care workers have become infected by COVID 19. 80% of COVID infected patients have fevers, and with hospitals have been overwhelmed, these symptomatic patients can easily infect healthcare workers who in turn see dozens of people during a shift.

Varna provides an enterprise approach that helps hospitals screen visitors who have high temperature and actively monitor staff temperature and overall health in order to prevent the spread of COVID-19. Our simple and affordable system uses AI on thermal cameras and a wearable biosensor to reimagine health screening to save lives.

Frequently Asked Questions

How to use Varna?
  • STEP 1. Healthcare workers(HW) enter their symptoms in the Varna app before coming for work.
  • STEP 2. At the hospitals, staff show the QR code generated by VarnaBot and have selfie station temperature taken.
  • STEP 3. If the temperature is below a threshold, they are allowed to enter the hospital and given a wearable chest strap during their shift.
  • STEP 4. The chest strap will buzz three times if their temperature is extremely high, a message will be sent through the app to notify them to rescreen immediately.
  • STEP 5. Manager gets notified when a staff member needs to go home.
  • STEP 6. Manager schedules the available backup who is notified in a message and an email that they need to come in at x time.
